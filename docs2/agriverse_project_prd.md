# AgriVerse: Digital Agriculture Pipeline PRD

## 1. Project Overview
AgriVerse is a production-ready digital agriculture platform designed to transform African farmers from novices into "Verified, Credit-Ready, and Bankable Entrepreneurs." The platform serves as a digital pipeline combining education, AI-powered assistance, secure commerce, and financial inclusion.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Beginner Farmers:** Individuals looking to build foundational knowledge and enter the agricultural market.
*   **Established Farmers:** Experienced professionals seeking to scale operations, access financing, and reach new markets.
*   **Stakeholders:** Financial institutions, investors, government partners, and enterprise buyers.

## 3. Product Vision: The Digital Pipeline
The user journey follows a structured path to value:
1.  **Smart Onboarding:** Personalized pathways based on initial skill level.
2.  **Education & Verification:** 3MTT-aligned training modules leading to digital badges and professional verification.
3.  **Intelligence Layer:** 24/7 AI assistance (Google Gemini) for crop detection, weather advisory, and market intelligence.
4.  **Financial Layer:** Transaction history and platform engagement used to generate a "Credit Readiness Score" for startup and expansion loans.
5.  **Market Access:** Secure B2B/B2C marketplace with OPay Escrow protection.

## 4. Key Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Education & Learning (3MTT Powered)
*   Modular learning paths for Beginners vs. Established farmers.
*   Automated issuance of Digital Badges upon course completion.
*   Verification system to transition users to "Verified Farmer" status.

### 4.2 AI Assistant (Google Gemini)
*   **Multilingual Support:** English, Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, and Nigerian Pidgin.
*   **Crop Disease Detection:** Image upload with instant diagnosis and treatment plans.
*   **Advisory:** Real-time planting alerts and harvest timing.

### 4.3 Marketplace & Payments
*   Buyer/Seller matching for agricultural inputs and produce.
*   **OPay Escrow:** Funds held until buyer confirms delivery to eliminate fraud.
*   Live market trends and wholesale pricing data.

### 4.4 Financial Services
*   **Credit Readiness Score:** Calculated based on Professional (3MTT), Transaction (OPay), Reputation (Ratings), and AI Engagement scores.
*   **Loan Products:** "Startup Loan" (seeds/setup) and "Expansion Loan" (mechanization/transport).

## 5. Design & Brand Identity

### 5.1 Visual Language
*   **Primary Color:** Emerald Green (#1FA971) - Growth and success.
*   **Navigation:** Deep Forest (#0F3D2E) - Professionalism and stability.
*   **Accent:** Gold (#F4B740) - Exclusively for high-value financial milestones (Credit, Loans, Badges).
*   **AI:** Gemini Blue (#2F80ED) - Modern intelligence.
*   **Typography:** Inter (400, 500, 600, 700) for clarity and modern SaaS feel.

### 5.2 Component Philosophy
*   **High Trust:** Clean borders, generous whitespace, and banking-grade security indicators.
*   **Authenticity:** Use of authentic African agriculture photography rather than illustrations.
*   **Efficiency:** 8-point grid system with consistent 16px card radii and 12px button radii.

## 6. Technical Specifications
*   **Platforms:** Responsive Desktop Web, Mobile Web, and Native Mobile App (390px base).
*   **Tech Integrations:** Google Gemini (AI), OPay (Payments/Escrow), 3MTT (Curriculum).
*   **Security:** Bank-grade encryption and verified user profiles.

## 7. Roadmap & Scope
1.  **Phase 1 (Current):** Foundation, Onboarding, and Core Dashboard.
2.  **Phase 2:** Learning Hub and AI Assistant integration.
3.  **Phase 3:** Marketplace, OPay Escrow, and Credit Scoring.
4.  **Phase 4:** Loan Application flows and Admin/Analytics Dashboards.
